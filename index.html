Background

Philadelphia has the greatest basketball scene in the country. Big 5. PCL. Tons of D2/D3. Opportunities to watch basketball are endless.

The purpose of this site is to compile all the local college games as well as some high school games, so you always know where you can catch a live basketball game in the Philadelphia area, without having to browse the apps/websites for all the teams. Just click the events you want to attend, and copy it to your personal calendar.

Note: I'll try to be on top of any updates for schedule changes, but I'd recommend—especially for D3/High School—to double check the athletic websites included in the event description before driving to the gym in case there were any last-minute changes.
